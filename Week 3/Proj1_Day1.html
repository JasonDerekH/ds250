Instead of filtering my dataset, I can change the years shown on the fly, in the plot

(
    ggplot(data = usa, mapping = aes(x = "Year", y= "Life")) +
    geom_line() +
    scale_x_continuous(limits = [1920, 2000]) +
    scale_y_continuous(limits = [50, 80])
)
